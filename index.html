This is an Odin fanpage
This website is dedicated to the norse God Odin and his attributes that are wisdom and poetry. He is the one-eyed principal God, also called Wotan, the father of Thor, a shape-shifter with his two ravens Huginn (thought) and Muninn (memory).

The attributes of Odin

Odin sacrificed his left eye to gain wisdom
Poetry, the runes and magic, Odin went great lengths to obtain them
Wanderer in disguise: An old man with a long beard, wearing a hat or hood
Huginn and Muninn, their names mean thought/idea and remembrance
Cattle die, kindred die, every man is mortal. But the good name never dies of one who has done well.
- Odin, God of Wisdom, taken from the Hávamál
